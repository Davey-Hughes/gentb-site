Contribute your data

You can add your tuberculosis data to genTB using the Harvard Dataverse Application. Datasets of both phenotype and genotype are most useful to the Tuberculosis community and we recommend contributing both if they are available.

If you upload FASTQ files that are paired end, please label them as follows:

  • isolate-name-here_R1.fastq
  • isolate-name-here_R2.fastq.

For a detailed guide on how to prepare your data for sharing please click here.

Steps:

  1. Create an account at dataverse.harvard.edu
  2. Go to the Tuberculosis Dataverse, click the Add Data button, and select New Dataset.
  3. Fill out the dataset deposit form, upload your files, and click Save Dataset.
  4. You are able to edit your dataset and upload new files after saving the dataset for the first time.
  5. Once your dataset is ready, click the Submit for Review button on the right side of the dataset page.
