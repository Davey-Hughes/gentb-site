Share contribute your data or download the current genTB data repository

The core dataset was last updated on 10/30/2015

You can add your tuberculosis data to genTB using the Harvard Dataverse Application. Datasets of both phenotype and genotype are most useful to the Tuberculosis community and we recommend contributing both if they are available. Please read the guide on how to prepare your data for sharing please click here.

Steps:

  1. Create an account at dataverse.harvard.edu
  2. Go to the genTB Dataverse, click the Add Data button, and select New Dataset.
  3. Fill out the dataset deposit form, upload your files, and click Save Dataset.
  4. You are able to edit your dataset and upload new files after saving the dataset for the first time.
  5. Once your dataset is ready, click the Submit for Review button on the right side of the dataset page.

Questions? Click the email button on the genTB Dataverse page.
