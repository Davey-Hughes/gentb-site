Translational Genomics of Tuberculosis

genTB is a genomics analysis tool for Mycobacterium tuberculosis (TB) data exploration and offers clinicians and researchers four main features: a seamless method to share data, the prediction of drug resistances based on genotype, geographic mapping, and a user friendly statistical analysis tool.
