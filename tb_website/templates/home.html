Translational Genomics of Tuberculosis

genTB is a genomics based analysis tool for Mycobacterium tuberculosis data exploration that offers four main features: a means for sharing, citing and crediting TB data and metadata, the prediction of resistance based on genotype, geographic data mapping, and a user friendly statistical analysis tool.
